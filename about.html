Who We Are:

STEM Model United Nations, founded in 2017 by STEM October Students. STEM MUN exposes students to the details of diplomacy at an early age.

It is a platform for future professionals to analyze diplomatic issues from a close proximity and test their knowledge in practical ways to explore new pathways not subscribed in textbooks.

Our Mission:

Our mission is to promote and encourage awareness, understanding and acceptance of different people, cultures, beliefs, and attitudes by organizing, and promoting Model United Nations conferences.

We also aim to provide a healthy environment for constructive discussions through which, high school students can develop their researching, public speaking, debating, critical thinking and leadership skills. These are all driven from our belief in making our community a better, more sophisticated place.

Our Vision:

As STEM MUN community we will

- Encourage STEM students to discuss the community challenges, present their own ideas concerning their community’s future, and debate on its impact on global society.

- Provide the space for those important discussions, while innovating the traditional customs of MUN conferences, to allow for a more realistic simulation.

- Act as a platform through which the United Nations can engage the MUN community, share information and act as a source of inspiration. Deliver experiential learning opportunities for STEM students that are focused on the SDGs and that bring together diverse individuals from around the world.

- Host Student-planned and executed events that allow for further sharing and collaboration around the SDGs.
